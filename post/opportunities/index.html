  • Prospective graduate students
  • If you think you are interested in joining my lab, please email me before applying to determine if our research interests align. In your email, include brief statements describing (1) your research interests; (2) your academic background; and (3) why you are specifically interested in joining my lab. Detailed instructions and admission requirements to UK Department of Plant and Soil Sciences are available here. I also encourage prospective students to apply to graduate fellowships (e.g., the NSF GRFP, USDA NIFA fellowships for predoctoral, or Fulbright Scholar Program for international students), and can advise you regarding fellowship applications given sufficient time (4-6 weeks) before the application deadline.
  • Postdoc fellowships
  • Please email me with a description of your research interests and a CV if you are interested in joining my lab. I can help you apply to a range of external postdoc fellowship opportunities (e.g., USDA NIFA, NSF fellowships for postdoctoral, NASA, NOAA, and Ford Foundation fellowships). and UK-specific fellowships (the UK Postdoctoral Fellowships).
  • Undergraduates
